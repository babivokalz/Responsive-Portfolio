About Me

Hello and welcome to my page! As you can tell, my name is Wendy. I currently work in Quality Assurance at a Biopharmaceutilcal company in RTP. I have a 3 year old Golden Retriever named Bailey who loves to play fetch. I was born and raised in Boston, Massachusetts (Go Pats/Celtics/Red Sox/Bruins!) so it was really eye-opening when I first moved to North Carolina. Prior to my move to NC, I lived and worked in Copenhagen, Denmark. My company sent me there for a temporary work assignment and it was definitely the most memorable and best experience I've ever had; it was amazing to be able to immerse myself in a totally different country and culture.

I'm an amateur foodie so I really miss all the yummy food in Boston. One thing that I don't think I'll ever get used to is the humidity... especially the summer time! It's unbearable to be outside - even for my outdoor loving pupper. In my free time I like to play video games (mostly PC, or Nintendo Switch), experiment with different food recipies with my boyfriend, and I love to travel! I also love going to EDM events and festivals with my friends from Boston.
